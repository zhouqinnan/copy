Trivalent’s Gold Standard

Auric is designed to ensure critical data remains protected in the file system of any device. Through Auric’s unique process of encryption and shredding, secured data is rendered useless by unauthorized users. Upon authentication, the data is reconstituted and the information is restored to its original state.

With the flexibility of a software implementation, Auric integrates seamlessly with existing technology and is scalable to facilitate deployment in any environment. Its file-level fault tolerance component allows for continuous availability and data provisioning.
